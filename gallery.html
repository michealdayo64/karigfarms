About Us

Our Company

KARIG FARMS was established with an objective to produce high quality palm kernel oil with its side products. Our plant caters to growing demands for certified-sustainable crude palm kernel oil (CPKO) by specialty fats and oleochemicals manufacturers, whilst the palm kernel expeller by-product will serve as a quality feedstock for the local animal husbandry sector.
